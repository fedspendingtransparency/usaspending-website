Behind every dollar the U.S. government spends, there is a story. But how can you discover that story? What tools do you need to understand it? And how can you learn what this story means for you, your family, your business, your community?

Is this story kept a secret? Can only certain people access this government spending data?

Thankfully, that story is easier than ever to discover. And anyone can discover it!

With the launch of *Exploring America’s Finances*, we invite you to take a closer look at how government funds are collected, spent, and managed.

The data isn’t a secret. In fact, the door is wide open.

Two key platforms – <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://usaspending.gov">USAspending.gov</a> and <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://fiscaldata.treasury.gov">FiscalData.Treasury.gov</a> – offer full access to federal financial data directly from the source. *No subscriptions, no cost, and no barriers*.

- Fiscal Data provides a big-picture view of how the government manages its money

- USAspending allows you to dive deep into where the money goes

Whether you’re a student, journalist, government employee, or curious citizen, these sites are your access point to a clearer understanding of America’s finances.

We believe that government transparency strengthens trust, and knowledge empowers people. So, we invite you to dive in, explore the data, and see for yourself.

Discover how your government spends money on the things that matter to you. Search <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://usaspending.gov">USAspending.gov</a> and <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://fiscaldata.treasury.gov">FiscalData.Treasury.gov</a> today!
