When you hear the term federal spending, you may think of big-ticket items like military, healthcare, or social services. But did you know that every year, federal spending helps protect the places that tell America’s story?

From the Washington Monument in Washington, D.C., to Yellowstone National Park, federal funding ensures these landmarks remain standing strong for generations to come.

Federal dollars flow primarily through agencies like the [National Park Service](https://www.usaspending.gov/agency/department-of-the-interior?fy=2025), which oversees more than 400 national parks, monuments, historic sites, and recreation areas. Spending covers everything from routine maintenance (such as repairing trails, visitor centers, and restrooms) to large-scale preservation projects that safeguard iconic structures from weathering and age.

![Washington Monument](preserving-americas-story.png)

Investments in these sites also support education by offering millions of visitors endless opportunities to learn about history, democracy, and natural heritage. They drive local economies as well. In a 2023 study, the National Park Service [reported](https://www.doi.gov/pressreleases/national-parks-contributed-record-high-556-billion-us-economy-supported-415000-jobs) that visitor spending at parks contributes tens of billions of dollars annually to the U.S. economy and supports hundreds of thousands of jobs nationwide.

Exploring USAspending.gov is a great way to answer questions such as: How much funding goes towards maintenance or restoration projects? Which states receive the most investment in their parks and cultural sites?

Here are a couple of [search](https://www.usaspending.gov/search) options to get you started:

- [Department of the Interior (DOI) Agency Profile](https://www.usaspending.gov/agency/department-of-the-interior?fy=2025): view the FY 2025 summary, status of funds, award spending, and more
-	[Department of the Interior Funding Search](https://www.usaspending.gov/search?hash=995eb3c79dc13ce141369db5e3358bca): view all funding to DOI
-	[Keyword Search](https://www.usaspending.gov/search?hash=55d6c05a49b4ece1de5c14f4d6dbffc3): Use Advanced Search keywords such as “Washington Monument”

The next time you visit a national park or monument, remember that you can trace the funding behind its care and preservation at [USAspending.gov](https://www.usaspending.gov). You might be surprised at what you find, and if you share what you learn with the park rangers or tour guides, you might impress them too!
